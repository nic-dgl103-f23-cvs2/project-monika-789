Courtenay Appliances has been a recognized name in the heart of the Comox Valley for over 30 years. Locally owned and operated, we offer a wide range of innovative, premier & specialty kitchen and laundry appliances to builders, designers, and homeowners. We carry over 57+ brands many world renowned such as Wolf SubZero, Bosch, Thermador, Jenn Air, Fisher & Paykel, Smeg and Whirlpool. Here assist in creating unique kitchen experience shop the appliances in our always changing showroom. We offer competitive pricing and take pride in our hassle-free after purchase care. Our team is dedicated to helping you find the perfect ingredients to cook up your dream kitchen!
